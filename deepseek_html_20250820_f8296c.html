Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical web project take?
The timeline for web projects varies based on complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while e-commerce sites and web applications can take 4-8 weeks or more. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.
Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, we offer various support packages to meet your needs. These include technical support, content updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization. We'll discuss options with you before project completion.
Can you work with our existing design or branding?
Absolutely. We can incorporate your existing branding materials, color schemes, and design elements into your new website or digital strategy. If you need design assistance, our team can help refresh or create new branding elements.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit with the remainder due upon completion. Monthly marketing services are billed monthly.
Do you provide hosting services?
We offer reliable hosting solutions optimized for performance and security. Alternatively, we can help you set up hosting with a provider of your choice. Our team will ensure your site is properly configured regardless of where it's hosted.
